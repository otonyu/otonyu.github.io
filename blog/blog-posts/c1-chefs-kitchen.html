Chefs in the Kitchen!

After watching Shereef's Fireside Chat, I think I gained a clarification of what DBC is and a greater appreciation for what they're doing. For me, DBC essentially boils down to passionate people, instructors and students, coming together to work towards a common goal: creating world class beginners! I think I will fit into DBC culture pretty seamlessly for a few reasons:

  • I'm not afraid to be a "chef". DBC seems like it is what you make of it, and want to make an amazing dish!
  • I'm willing to delve as deep as I can into the program and get everything I can out of it, without stepping on anyone's toes of course.
  • Honestly, so far this process has been really exciting. Challenging, but exciting. I can't get enough. Going to bed late, waking up early (within reason :)). It's been exhilarating since day 1!
I can't wait to see what else is in store!
